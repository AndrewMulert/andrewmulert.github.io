Different Hobbies, Activites, and Media that I enjoy

Interests

A few of the stories, games,

movies, and activites that are my favorites.

Infinity Train

Infinity Train is one of my favorite shows due to it's good character development through the show, the beautiful animation, and the emphasis it puts on change. This show has inspired many hours of designing and drawing, and excites me with the unique storytelling and mysteries it presents it's audience with. While the show may have been cancelled, I hope for it's return to television someday.

Star Wars

Star Wars is an inspiration to millions around the world about the wonders of the future and the joys of Technology and Science. The stories give hope and have helped me to endure instead of giving up, whether that's on a project or just life in general. Star Wars will be something I continue to appreciate, for the excitement it has and will give me in the future.

Marvel

Marvel has inspired me in many ways, but one of the most important ways is that anything is possible. There are many types of people in the world, and it's important to recognize the influence you have on the lives of others. When you look at yourself, you need to determine for yourself if you are going to choose to help others or not every day of your life. Marvel reminds me that these choices and changes can happen, but that's up to you if you choose to make those actions.
